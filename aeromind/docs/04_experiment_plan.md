# Experimental Plan

## Participants
- Number: approximately 5 (team members only)
- Characteristics: variation in hand size, shape, and appearance
- No personal identifying data collected

## Gesture Set
1. Takeoff
2. Land
3. Move forward
4. Move backward
5. Rotate left
6. Emergency stop

All gestures are static and identical across participants. The emergency stop gesture has the highest priority.

## Experimental Conditions

### Lighting
- Normal indoor lighting
- Low indoor lighting

### Background
- Clean background
- Cluttered background

### Distance
- Near (~0.5 m from camera)
- Far (~1.0 m from camera)

## Procedure
- Each participant performs each gesture under all conditions
- Gestures are held stable for a fixed duration
- System logs predictions, confidence values, commands, and timestamps
- Safety behavior is observed and recorded

## Environment
- Indoor, controlled space
- Consumer Wi-Fi connection
- Laptop-based gesture recognition
